Vegan Muesli

Delicous Vegan Muesli

Muesli is a cold oatmeal dish based on rolled oats. Was traditionally prepared with mild but in this case we`re going to prepare a vegan version.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups rolled oats
  • 1 ½(14 ounce) cans coconut milk, or more to taste
  • 3 tablespoons chia seeds
  • 2 granny smith apples, peeled and grated
  • ½ lemon, juiced
  • 2 cups sliced strawberries
  • 1 cup blueberries
  • 2 tablespoons maple syrup, or to taste

Directions

  1. Step 1

    Mix oats, coconut milk, and chia seeds together in a bowl.

  2. Step 2

    Toss apples and lemon juice together in a bowl until coated; fold into oat mixture. Stir strawberries, blueberries, and maple syrup into oat mixture. Refrigerate for 8 hours to overnight.
